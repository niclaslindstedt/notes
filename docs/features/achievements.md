# Achievements

Every feature in the app is also an unlockable trophy. As you use it — writing your first note, switching themes, connecting a folder or the cloud, turning on encryption — you quietly earn achievements that mark how far you've grown into the app. They're sorted into four tiers that climb with you: **Beginner**, **Intermediate**, **Pro**, and **Expert**.

You don't chase them; they find you. The moment an action unlocks something new, the trophy button in the header lights up with a badge.

## How it works

1. **Use the app as you normally would.** Each feature you reach for the first time unlocks its matching trophy in the background.
2. **Watch the header trophy.** When you have new, unacknowledged unlocks it shows a badge. Tapping it opens a notification listing just the ones you've earned, and closing that clears the badge.
3. **Browse the full tour.** When there's nothing new waiting, the same trophy button opens a guided tour of the whole catalog — every trophy across all four tiers, locked and unlocked, so you can see what's still ahead.

You can switch achievements off entirely from **Settings → General**; the trophy button disappears and tracking stops. Trophies you've already earned are kept, so turning it back on shows the same progress.

Your progress isn't tied to one device. When you turn on cloud sync, the trophies you've earned travel with you — they ride along in the same synced settings as your theme and font choices — so the tour looks the same on your phone as it does on your laptop.
