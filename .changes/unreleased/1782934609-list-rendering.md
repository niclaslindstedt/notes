---
type: Changed
title: Sharper list and divider rendering
---

Ordered lists now number themselves sequentially (1., 2., 3.) whatever digits you type, nested lists indent and rotate their markers by depth (numeric → a. → i. for numbered, • → ◦ → ▪ for bulleted, the top-level bullet drawn a little larger and vertically centred on its line), and a line that is just a single `-` renders as a horizontal divider.
