---
type: Changed
title: Image viewer gallery, swipe-to-dismiss, and tidy previews
---

The full-size image viewer is now a gallery — swipe up or down (or tap the X, the backdrop, or Escape) to dismiss, and step through a note's images with the on-screen arrows, the arrow keys, or a left/right swipe, with the neighbouring images peeking in at the edges on a wide screen the way Finder does; deleting an image from a note now also removes its underlying attachment, and image markdown no longer clutters a note's preview in the overview.
