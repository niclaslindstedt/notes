---
type: Fixed
title: Undo and redo are scoped to the open note
---

Undo/redo (the buttons and ⌘/Ctrl+Z / ⌘/Ctrl+Y) now walk the history of the note you're actually looking at, so switching notes no longer reverts edits in a different one, and erasing what you typed before typing something else is now its own step instead of being swallowed by the next edit.
