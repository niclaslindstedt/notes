type: Added
title: Undo and redo
---

Creating, editing, and deleting notes is now recorded on an undo timeline — step back and forth with Ctrl/⌘+Z (and Ctrl/⌘+Shift+Z or Ctrl+Y to redo) or the new Undo / Redo entries in the side menu, so an accidental deletion or a regretted change is one tap away from coming back.
